# Places

## The Five Shires

An area almost entirely populated by hobbits. Mountains to the north (home of goblins who drove out the dwarves), The Great Woods to the east (elves and fey), The Swamp to the west, The Sea to the south. Five shires in the confederation: Eastshire (wilder, elf-adjacent), Highshire (highlands, shepherds), Seashire (wealthy, industrious coast), Southshire (least prosperous, butt of jokes), and Heartshire (central, pastoral, traditional—where the campaign takes place). No real government; loose confederation of families and townships. Ancient giant ruins dot the landscape, mostly repurposed for stone. Hobbits are ~3' tall, dwarves and goblins ~4', elves up to ~5'. Humans ("tallfolk") are mythical grotesque creatures in hobbit stories. Elves are seen as dangerous alien tricksters (Terry Pratchett's take). The Stonecleft dwarves are the dwarves the Toad Stompers have encountered.

### Locations

- **The Heart Road:** Main road connecting [Thistledown](#thistledown), The Right Way Inn, and [Gomwick](#gomwick).
- **The Old Road:** Forest road between [Thistledown](#thistledown) and [Gomwick](#gomwick). [Farmer Fallow](/hobbity/world/npcs/#farmer-fallow)'s stall, strange mists and happenings.
- **Sunnyside Crossroads:** Where the main road turns south. Huddle Farm is just north.
- **Huddle Farm:** A hobbit farmstead near the Sunnyside Crossroads, en route to [Orlane](#orlane). The hill home sits above ancient ruins. [Tom Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#tom-huddle) is expanding into the hill and repurposing stones from the Fortfield ruins—some decorated with serpent motifs. Adjacent to the [Sutton](/hobbity/world/npcs/#norrie-sutton) farm, separated by a disputed hedge along Deadman's Gap. Notable NPCs: [Tom Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#tom-huddle), [Primula Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#primula-huddle), [Motto Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#motto-huddle), [Otto Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#otto-huddle), [Berry Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#berry-huddle), [Sherry Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#sherry-huddle), [Sara Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#sara-huddle), [Mary Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#mary-huddle), [Bordo Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#bordo-huddle), [Marlo Huddle](/hobbity/world/npcs/#marlo-huddle), [Krund Pothrower](/hobbity/world/npcs/#krund-pothrower), [Epsten](/hobbity/world/npcs/#epsten), [Norrie Sutton](/hobbity/world/npcs/#norrie-sutton), [Sam Sutton](/hobbity/world/npcs/#sam-sutton). Fortfield Tower: crumbling ancient wizard's tower with five explored basement levels.
- **The Right Way Inn:** An inn on the Heart Road between [Gomwick](#gomwick) and [Thistledown](#thistledown). More of a small village than an inn—lively, boisterous, with a communal Long Room and private rooms.

## Gomwick

<MapPreview slug="gomwick" />

A proper hobbit town with a harvest festival, shops, and the estate of [Buford Niss](/hobbity/world/npcs/#buford-niss) on Toppo Hill overlooking it all. The Heart Road stretches eastward toward The Right Way Inn and [Thistledown](#thistledown).

## Orlane

<MapPreview slug="orlane" />

A hobbit village on a flat agricultural plain in one of the richest and most fertile agricultural areas of the eastern shire. Most buildings are above ground (hobbit-holes are hard to build on flat land). A river divides the village—the affected side is [Bertram](/hobbity/world/npcs/#bertram)'s. People disappear or come back "changed" ([Mattie Snowvale](/hobbity/world/npcs/#mattie-snowvale) became violent after his disappearance and return).

### Notable NPCs

[Constable Grover](/hobbity/world/npcs/#constable-grover), [Belba](/hobbity/world/npcs/#belba), [Olwyn](/hobbity/world/npcs/#olwyn), [Zacharias](/hobbity/world/npcs/#zacharias), [Killian Gade](/hobbity/world/npcs/#killian-gade), [Vilma](/hobbity/world/npcs/#vilma), [Rolo](/hobbity/world/npcs/#rolo), [Maddie Snowvale](/hobbity/world/npcs/#maddie-snowvale), [Bertram](/hobbity/world/npcs/#bertram), [Quinn Finla](/hobbity/world/npcs/#quinn-finla), [Cirilli Finla](/hobbity/world/npcs/#cirilli-finla), [Wilbur Oldbuck](/hobbity/world/npcs/#wilbur-oldbuck), [Traver Stokehart](/hobbity/world/npcs/#traver-stokehart), [Ramne](/hobbity/world/npcs/#ramne), [Abramo](/hobbity/world/npcs/#abramo)

### Locations

- **The Slumbering Serpent Inn:** The Toad Stompers' base of operations. Run by [Belba](/hobbity/world/npcs/#belba) and [Olwyn](/hobbity/world/npcs/#olwyn).
- **The Foaming Mug Inn:** Abandoned inn with signs of violent struggle. Troglodyte nest in the basement.
- **The Golden Grain Inn:** Burned down. Owned by [Bertram](/hobbity/world/npcs/#bertram). The cult of Explictica used its tunnels to move abducted families out of town for charming rituals. Set on fire from inside after [Zacharias](/hobbity/world/npcs/#zacharias) and [Derek](/hobbity/world/npcs/#derek-desleigh) fled there.
- **The Temple of Merikka:** New temple behind three-meter walls. Dedicated to a goddess of farming, fertility, and light. Hides something much older beneath it.
- **The Dungeon Beneath the Temple:** Ancient ruins predating the temple. Two levels: upper with troglodyte lair and underground stream; lower with [Abramo](/hobbity/world/npcs/#abramo)'s serpent cult lair, torture chamber, goblin barracks, and a jade naga statue of the goddess Explictica.
- **Ramne's Cottage:** Half-buried stone cottage west of the Slumbering Serpent. Home of [Ramne](/hobbity/world/npcs/#ramne).

## Thistledown

Home hamlet of all three PCs in the [Five Shires](#the-five-shires). A small, quiet hobbit settlement southeast of [Gomwick](#gomwick), off the Heart Road. The Toad Stompers set out from here with Boffo's Uncle Bungo's pony Binny, heading to the [Gomwick](#gomwick) Harvest Festival.
