Buford sent the party to Orlane to investigate a letter from an old adventurer. They found a village where people vanish and come back wrong, a new temple hiding something ancient, and a cult of the serpent goddess Explictica spreading toward Gomwick. The party killed the cult's priest Abramo beneath the Temple of Merikka, freed a prisoner, and emerged to find the Golden Grain Inn ablaze and Zacharias fled with Derek Desleigh. Orlane is fractured. The tunnels run deeper than anyone knew.
