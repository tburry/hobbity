With the temple cleared, its priest dead, and the Golden Grain Inn a smoking ruin, the Toad Stompers turn from the village to the cult's road out of it. Ramne the old druid points them northwest—past the Dimwood, into a swamp called the Rushmoors—and lends them his snake-killing weasel. Beneath the burnt inn they find the entrance: a hot, low, hand-dug troglodyte tunnel that has been spiriting families out of Orlane for months. Somewhere down it, something is waiting for them.
