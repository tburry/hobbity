Three young hobbits set out from Thistledown with a pony and a plan: sell goods at the Gomwick Harvest Festival, eat well, and be home by week's end. What they found instead was a strange mist in the woods, a rampaging scarecrow, a dead man in a hermit's cabin, and prophetic visions that followed them home into winter. By spring, they could wield magic, and an old hobbit's letter was calling them back to the road.
