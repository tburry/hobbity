The leader, whether anyone asked for one or not. Third-generation nickname (Bill Wedgerton's axe was called "Thin Edge of the Wedge"). He drives decisions, handles NPC diplomacy, and frames the tactical questions. Slightly too confident in his own read on every situation, but owns it when he's wrong. Carries an ESP scroll he hasn't told the others about. Wedge has the Zacharias-to-Buford letter in his possession.

## Spells

- **Cantrip:** Fireflies—conjure 1d4 fireflies that fly around your hand for 3 blinks before fading
- **Level 1:** Light

- **Animal whistle**—from [Farmer Fallow](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#farmer-fallow)'s stall. Calls an animal when blown.
- **Ring of Protection +1**—taken from [Misha](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#misha). Bonus to armor and saves.

## Voice

Wedge talks the most and the fastest. He's affectionately impatient with his companions, always ten feet ahead and annoyed about it. His confidence bumps into reality regularly (sneaking with a lit lantern) but he pushes forward anyway. In a crisis he leads with spirit, not caution. Calls the party "the Toad Stompers." Uses "fellas" and "guys" constantly.

### Sample Lines

- "Might have to put a rope around these fellas, with all this lollygagging."
- "Their plan will not survive first contact with the Toad Stompers."
- "We've been into the dungeon underneath your bedroom and killed your troglodytes and killed your goblins and we want to know what's going on. So no, we won't put our weapons down."
- "Come on, Turnip. Toad Stompers!"
