# Locations

## The Five Shires

An area almost entirely populated by hobbits. Mountains to the north (home of goblins who drove out the dwarves), The Great Woods to the east (elves and fey), The Swamp to the west, The Sea to the south. Five shires in the confederation: Eastshire (wilder, elf-adjacent), Highshire (highlands, shepherds), Seashire (wealthy, industrious coast), Southshire (least prosperous, butt of jokes), and Heartshire (central, pastoral, traditional — where the campaign takes place). No real government; loose confederation of families and townships. Ancient giant ruins dot the landscape, mostly repurposed for stone. Hobbits are ~3' tall, dwarves and goblins ~4', elves up to ~5'. Humans ("tallfolk") are mythical grotesque creatures in hobbit stories. Elves are seen as dangerous alien tricksters (Terry Pratchett's take). The Stonecleft dwarves are the dwarves the party has encountered.

The party set out from here with Uncle's pony [Opal](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#opal), heading to the [Gomwick](#gomwick) Harvest Festival via the [Old Road](#the-old-road) (took the forest road instead of the safer North Road after a late departure).

## The Right Way Inn

## Gomwick

A proper hobbit town with a harvest festival, shops, and the estate of [Buford Niss](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#buford-niss) on Toppo Hill overlooking it all. The Heart Road stretches eastward toward [The Right Way Inn](#the-right-way-inn) and [Thistledown](#thistledown).

[Buford Niss](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#buford-niss), [Dalton Dallydown](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#dalton-dallydown), [Gammy Boffin](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#gammy-boffin), [Daisy Boffin](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#daisy-boffin), [Lotho Longbuck](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#lotho-longbuck), [Adelard Potts](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#adelard-potts), [Hanna Boggs](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#hanna-boggs), [Grunela Bunce](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#grunela-bunce), [Boris Duddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#boris-duddle)

### Happenings

Boffo won the pie-eating semi-final (defeated [Grunela Bunce](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#grunela-bunce), the favourite). Wedge scored two bullseyes in axe-throwing. The party foiled a poisoning plot ([Adelard Potts](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#adelard-potts) and [Hanna Boggs](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#hanna-boggs) using [Tobias Chubb](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#tobias-chubb) as agent) and survived a rampaging scarecrow ("Hungry Hank" — came to life via magic). [Buford](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#buford-niss) hired them to investigate [Orlane](#orlane). Party earned 310xp each from the giant toad fight (Turnip's sleep dart took out 80% of its HP).

## Sunnyside Crossroads

Where the main road turns south. [Huddle Farm](#huddle-farm) is just north. The farthest the party has ever been from home.

## Huddle Farm

A hobbit farmstead near the [Sunnyside crossroads](#sunnyside-crossroads), en route to [Orlane](#orlane). The hill home sits above ancient ruins. [Tom](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#tom-huddle) is expanding into the hill (not unusual for prosperous hobbits) and repurposing stones from the [Fortfield](#fortfield-tower) ruins — some decorated with serpent motifs. Adjacent to the [Sutton](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#norrie-sutton) farm, separated by the disputed hedge along Deadman's Gap.

### Notable NPCs

[Tom Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#tom-huddle), [Primula Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#primula-huddle), [Motto Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#motto-huddle), [Otto Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#otto-huddle), [Berry Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#berry-huddle), [Sherry Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#sherry-huddle), [Sara Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#sara-huddle), [Mary Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#mary-huddle), [Bordo Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#bordo-huddle), [Marlo Huddle](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#marlo-huddle), [Krund Pothrower](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#krund-pothrower), [Epsten](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#epsten), [Norrie Sutton](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#norrie-sutton), [Sam Sutton](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#sam-sutton)

### Happenings

The party investigated escalating sabotage in the Huddle-[Sutton](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#norrie-sutton) feud (30gp job): dog kidnapped, vegetable garden destroyed, wine stolen (likely [Krund](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#krund-pothrower)), barn burned (accidentally by [Berry](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#berry-huddle) and [Sam](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#sam-sutton)), cows painted green. Explored five basement levels of the [Fortfield Tower](#fortfield-tower) (old wizard's tower): B1 bedroom/paint evidence, B2 workshop/crab spider, B3 library/crystal security system, B4 summoning room/"The Eye" (floating eye trap), B5 tunnel to well caverns. Found Nailer the dog dead in a camouflaged well, dissolved by Green Slime. Poisoned toads surrounding the well. Locked treasure chest left unopened — [Tom](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#tom-huddle)'s unexplained wealth remains a question.

## Fortfield Tower

A crumbling ancient wizard's tower on the [Huddle Farm](#huddle-farm) property, surrounded by scattered ruins. [Tom](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#tom-huddle) plans to level it all and bury the ruins under excavation debris. A tarp-camouflaged well nearby was surrounded by dead (poisoned) toads.

### Happenings

The party explored five basement levels:

- **B1:** Bedroom/living quarters. Green paint and empty paint buckets found (evidence of cow-painting). A painted warning about "The Eye."
- **B2:** Workshop and laboratory. Killed a crab spider.
- **B3:** Library. Fought the wizard's magical crystal security system (AC4, 12hp — Boffo clobbered it for 7).
- **B4:** Summoning room. "The Eye" — a floating eye trap that rotated and discharged elemental attacks (lightning, fire, poison, cold) on a timer. More puzzle than fight.
- **B5:** Unfinished room with a natural tunnel connecting to caverns under the well. A locked treasure room contained two empty chests, broken chests, and one heavy locked full chest (left unopened). Nailer the dog found dead in the well, dissolved by Green Slime.

## Orlane

A hobbit village on a flat agricultural plain in one of the richest and most fertile agricultural areas of the eastern shire. Most buildings are above ground (hobbit-holes are hard to build on flat land). A river divides the village — the affected side is [Bertram](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#bertram)'s. People disappear or come back "changed" (the blacksmith became violent after his disappearance and return). A new [temple to Merikka](#the-temple-of-merikka) sits on one of the few hills, behind three-meter walls. Early settlers tried to dig into the hill but gave up quickly.

### Notable NPCs

[Constable Grover](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#constable-grover), [Belba](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#belba), [Olwyn](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#olwyn), [Zacharias](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#zacharias), [Killian Gade](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#killian-gade), [Vilma](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#vilma), [Rolo](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#rolo), [Maddie Snowvale](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#maddie-snowvale), [Bertram](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#bertram), [Quinn Finla](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#quinn-finla), [Wilbur Oldbuck](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#wilbur-oldbuck), [Traver Stokehart](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#traver-stokehart)

### Happenings

The party investigated the village over several days. Day 1: arrived, met [Grover](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#constable-grover), reported dire wolves. Day 2: used magic on the blacksmith (sleep dart), upsetting his wife [Maddie](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#maddie-snowvale); Grover told them to conclude their business and leave. Night 2: skulked through backyards, broke into [Zacharias](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#zacharias)'s house (lit candles everywhere), broke into the [Foaming Mug](#the-foaming-mug-inn) ruins. Day 3: caught by Grover and deputies exiting the Foaming Mug. Found troglodytes nesting in the basement (three pallets, two killed, one unaccounted for). Rebuffed at [Temple of Merikka](#the-temple-of-merikka) gates.

## The Foaming Mug Inn

Supposedly abandoned because the village could no longer sustain three inns. The usual story is the owners packed up and left. In reality, the place was "Dripping Daggered" — signs of violent struggle inside. A troglodyte nest in the basement (three sleeping pallets).

### Happenings

The party found signs of violent struggle and two troglodytes sleeping on pallets in the basement. Killed both. A third pallet was empty — unaccounted for.

## The Temple of Merikka

A new temple on one of the few hills in [Orlane](#orlane), behind imposing three-meter walls. Dedicated to Merikka — a goddess of farming, fertility, home and the hearth, knowledge, and light. Described as "a bringer of light and illumination." Ironically similar to Chauntea and Amaunator combined. The original temple on this site was to a "long-forgotten god." Early settlers tried to build homes into the hill but gave up quickly. The priests and devotees no longer come into the village. Two priests from the coast plus followers. Behind it lies something much older. The DM has hinted: "you're standing in the shadow of a great big rebuilt temple to some goddess of 'Illumination' who seeks to bring the light back into the world. Go buy some sunglasses."

### Notable NPCs

[Misha](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#misha), [Derek Desleigh](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#derek-desleigh), [Griff Snowvale](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#griff-snowvale-gardner), [Timba Snowvale](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#timba-snowvale-gardner), [Kaylin Snowvale](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#kaylin-snowvale)

### Happenings

The party entered posing as pilgrims. Got [Misha](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#misha) to heal Boffo (critical success on the request), then ransacked her room — took her armor, shield, and mace. They fought [Derek](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#derek-desleigh) and goblins in the worship hall (Derek wanted the party taken alive). Found golden food offerings in a hallway (each worth ~5000gp) — Turnip did NOT pick up the golden carrot despite wanting to (DM confirmed). This will be resolved next session with saving throws, potentially with dire consequences. In the March 22 session, they subdued [Griff](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#griff-snowvale-gardner) and [Timba](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#timba-snowvale-gardner) (villagers from Orlane, cultists) but Misha (hit with sling, failed morale, fled in her nightgown), [Kaylin](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#kaylin-snowvale), and Derek escaped. Boffo touched a golden potato and had a vision of Merikka underwater.

The skeleton fight was the critical error: the party moved 5 feet too far back from the doorway, allowing two skeletons to enter simultaneously instead of fighting them one at a time in the bottleneck. Turnip went down (Brooch of Shielding absorbed 30+ damage before shattering), was revived but diminished. Boffo fell next, dying, with no healing left. The party had enough loot and prisoners to have retreated to [Grover](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#constable-grover)/[Zacharias](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#zacharias), leveled up, and returned with reinforcements — classic "one more room" syndrome.

## The Dungeon Beneath the Temple of Merikka

Ancient ruins predating the [Temple of Merikka](#the-temple-of-merikka) above. A mix of constructed corridors with iron spikes, natural cave, and new internal walls creating a maze. Connected to an underground stream that likely reaches the river.

### Notable NPCs

[Goblin Four](/hobbity/appendix/npcs/#goblin-four)

### Happenings

The party explored troglodyte-clawed caves with prison cells. Killed a giant lizard. Found the body of the real priestess in a crawlspace. Discovered a document naming [Orlane](#orlane) as a target and [Gomwick](#gomwick) as next. Fought waves of troglodytes in the stream caverns. Boffo drank a Potion of Growth and became giant-sized. Found 420 gold and the Brooch of Shielding in the troglodyte lair.
